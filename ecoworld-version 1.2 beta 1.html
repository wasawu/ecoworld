🏛️ Choose Your Economic System
This is the most important decision in EcoWorld.
Your economic system changes HOW you can produce, trade, and set prices.
💰
Capitalist
Private ownership. You set your own prices and keep all profits.
✅ +50% selling price
✅ Free market pricing
⚠️ No safety net — if you run out of money, no help
📈 Best for high risk / high reward
🏛️
Command Economy
The government controls what gets produced and at what price.
✅ Start with +50% resources
✅ Guaranteed trade deals
⚠️ Prices are fixed — can't exploit market spikes
📉 Slower wealth growth
⚖️
Mixed Economy
Combination of private and government control. Moderate rules, moderate rewards.
✅ Balanced bonuses
✅ Subsidies on some factories
⚖️ Medium price flexibility
🌍 Best for global trade
Real-world example: USA ≈ Capitalist  |  Cuba ≈ Command  |  Germany ≈ Mixed

Tutorial

1/10
🌾
Welcome to EcoWorld Market!
You chose a Mixed Economy. Now you'll build a town, produce goods, respond to markets, and trade globally — all while learning 5 real economics standards.
Watch the LIVE MARKET TICKER at the top — prices change as you sell. The LEFT PANEL shows your Labor, Capital, and Entrepreneurship in real time.
🕹️ WASD to walk  |  E near buildings to enter  |  ESC to close
🎓 TARGET 1 — Economic Systems, Sectors & Decisions
🏛️
Your Economic System Changes Everything
You picked a system that controls how freely you can buy, sell, and set prices. In a capitalist economy, you set prices freely. In a command economy, the government decides.
Economies have three sectors:
🌾 Primary — mining raw materials (your resource nodes)
🏭 Secondary — manufacturing goods (your factories)
📈 Tertiary — services like markets and trade
📋 TASK: Walk to 🏛️ Town Hall (center) and open it. Make a production decision by unlocking any factory!
🎓 TARGET 2 — Productive Resources (Factors of Production)
⛏️
You Need All Four Factors
🌍 Land — your natural resources (food, oil, wood, metal, tech)
👷 Labor — you start with workers; hire more at Town Hall
🏭 Capital — factories you unlock (shown in top HUD)
💡 Entrepreneurship — every upgrade decision you make
You cannot produce goods without enough Labor. If your workers are all busy, production stops! Hire more workers to expand.
📋 TASK: Go to 🌾 Farm (NW) — upgrade the Food node (Capital investment) and unlock Bread factory (requires Labor)!
🎓 TARGET 3 — Supply & Demand
📈
YOUR Actions Move Prices
Watch the live price ticker at the top. Every time you sell a good, its price drops slightly. When NPCs demand something and supply is low, prices spike.
Try this experiment:
1. Sell 5+ Bread at the Market
2. Watch the Bread price drop in the ticker
3. Now wait — NPC demand will push it back up!

You're watching supply & demand in real time.
📋 TASK: Go to 📈 Market (south) and sell goods. Then notice prices changing in the ticker!
🎓 TARGET 4 — Scarcity & Economic Reasoning
⚠️
Resources Are Limited — Choose Wisely
Scarcity: You only have limited Labor, limited resources, limited capital. You can't make everything at once.
Every choice has an opportunity cost:
"If I use my 4 workers to run the Bread factory, I can't also run Cars."

When a World Event hits (drought, oil spill), scarcity gets worse. You must adapt your strategy!
📋 TASK: Go to the 🔩 Foundry (SE) and unlock & produce a Car. It needs 3 Metal + 1 Tech + 1 Oil. Plan around your limited resources!
🎓 TARGET 5 — Specialization & Global Trade
🚢
Your Nation Has a Specialty Bonus
Each AI nation produces one resource cheaply. Your town also has a specialization bonus depending on your economic system. Trade to get what you can't produce efficiently.
🌿 Greendale — Food specialists (30% food rate bonus)
⚒️ Ironforge — Metal specialists (they want food!)
🔬 Techopolis — Tech specialists (they want oil!)

Without trade, you'd need 5× the resources to match their output.
📋 TASK: Visit the 🚢 Trade Port (west) and accept a trade deal with an AI nation!
🎓 ALL TARGETS — The Townspeople (Demand Side)

Citizens walk around with 💬 bubbles when they want goods. Their demand drives prices. More NPC demand = higher prices = more profit for you!
